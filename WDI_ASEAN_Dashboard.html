WDI Statistics ASEAN Dashboard

These dashboards provide insights on the selected world bank development indicators, and their comparison among ASEAN countries: Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Loas, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, and Viet Nam. A total of 26 indicators were included in this dashboard, displaying their average number, first and last date of the collected data. The audience can choose the type of indicators, ASEAN country, and specific year of their interest. The second dashboard highlights the trend of WDI indicators from the year of 1960 to the latest year. The data source is from https://databank.worldbank.org/source/world-development-indicators#.
