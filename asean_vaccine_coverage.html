ASEAN Vaccine Coverage Trends (2000-2023)

The dataset includes vaccination coverage data for 14 vaccines across 10 ASEAN countries, spanning the years 2000 to 2023. It consists of 124 records and 28 variables. The downloaded data was pivoted from long to wide format using 'melt'. The line graphs were visualized using 'seasborn'.
